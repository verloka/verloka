Tariscope Telecom

Call accounting software

The Tariscope is a telecom billing solution for telecommunications service providers of telephony and Internet. Also it is a call accounting software for companies that have PBX (VoIP PBX) systems.

It allows:

  • to keep accounting of call details from your PBXs
  • to manage and control costs on calls or IP traffic
  • to analyze the work of employees associated with the use of phones or the Internet
  • to automate the generation of call accounting reports on the schedule
  • to use a powerful subscriber data management system
  • to automatically generate invoices, receipts, details of services provided for each customer
  • flexibility to adapt to the requirements of your company
  • to carry out settlements with other telecom service providers
