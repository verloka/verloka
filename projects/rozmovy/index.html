Speech Recognition SaaS Solution

The solution is primarily intended for contact centers for the purpose of increasing productivity and indicators of the quality of customer service. The main feature of the solution is speech recognition (currently American English, German and Russian). This feature allows you to fire employees who listen to calls. Instead, the solution will do this and translate into text.

Other equally important functions

  • Reporting, including report designer.
  • Estimation of audio (and agents too).
  • Search audio in words or phrases.
  • Support for agent departments.
  • Agent Personal Area.
  • A full-fledged web application that allows you to manage them from anywhere in the World.
