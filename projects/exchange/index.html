Exchange CMS Social network

Q&A Engine

Exchange CMS is a stunning, professional, and flexible social questions and answers .NET application specially designed for Online Communities, Niche Questions and Answers Websites, Marketing Websites, Developers Websites, or any kind of Social Communities.

Important functions

  • Adaptive design.
  • User rating system.
  • Support for MS SQL, MySQL and PostgreSQL databases.
  • Website personalization for the user.
  • Content complaints system.
  • Complex caching mechanism improves performance on weak servers.
